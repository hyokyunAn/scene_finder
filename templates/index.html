Scene Finder: Video Scene Search

    - Quickly find the desired scene in a video

[How to Use]

  1. Upload the video file directly or enter a YouTube link.
  2. Write a description of the scene you want to find and press the search button. Descriptions can be made in various ways, such as depicting images, describing dialogues, or character descriptions.
  3. Scene Description Examples:
    • A man saying, 'Can I have a drink?'
    • A man running with a child on his back.
